# Foundation

`FOUNDATION.md` is the entry guide to the foundational law model.

## Foundation structure

The foundational corpus is in `foundation/` and is organized as:
- `axioms/`
- `invariants/`
- `boundaries/`
- `terminology/`
- `extensions/`

## Conceptual model

- Axioms define constitutional truths.
- Invariants define mandatory behavior constraints.
- Boundaries define authority/effect/cognition separation.
- Terminology defines canonical meaning and alias policy.

## Ontology baseline

Foundation semantics are anchored to:
- `core` sovereign plane
- `exec` external-effect plane
- `brain` governed cognitive plane
- `protocol/platform/support` as cross-cutting layers

Historical names are allowed only as declared aliases.

## Primacy rule

All runtime/contracts/registry/schema/formal artifacts are subordinate to the foundational corpus.
No lower surface may redefine foundational truth.
